# Design System: Wolters Contratacao

## Brand Identity
- **Primary Color (Blue)**: `#004F9F` (Deep Corporate Blue - InfoJobs style)
- **Secondary Color (Light Blue)**: `#E6F4FF` (Backgrounds/Accents)
- **Action Color (Orange/CTA)**: `#FF6B00` (High contrast CTA)
- **Neutral**: Slate/Gray scale for text (`#1e293b`, `#475569`)

## Typography
- **Font**: Inter or system-ui (Clean, modern sans-serif)
- **Headings**: Bold, dark slate (`text-slate-900`)
- **Body**: Readable slate (`text-slate-600`)

## Layout Principles
- **Container**: Max width 1200px (`max-w-6xl` or `max-w-7xl`)
- **Spacing**: Generous padding (`py-16`, `gap-8`)
- **Cards**: White background, subtle shadow (`shadow-sm` to `shadow-md`), rounded corners (`rounded-xl`)

## Components

### Hero
- Background: Blue gradient or clean white with blue accents.
- Content: Headline + Subhead + Primary CTA.

### Cards (Services/Info)
- Border: `border-slate-200`
- Hover: `hover:shadow-lg transition-all`

## Content
- **Logo**: "Wolters Contratacao" (Text + Icon if image missing)
- **Sections**:
    1. Hero (Value Prop)
    2. About Us (Trust)
    3. Services (Industrial sectors)
    4. International (European presence)
    5. Footer (Contact)
